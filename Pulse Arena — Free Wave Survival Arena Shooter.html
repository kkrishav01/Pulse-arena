About Pulse Arena

Pulse Arena drops you into an arena full of hostile drones that arrive in waves, getting tougher every round. Destroy drones to earn credits, then spend them in the Armory on 14 different weapons — from the starter Pulse Pistol up through the endgame Annihilator Beam — and upgrade each gun's damage and range independently. Every 5th wave, a boss blocks your path, cycling between three attack styles and growing stronger every time you face one.

How to play

WASD to move, mouse to aim and fire, Tab to open the Armory between waves, and number keys 1-9, 0 to switch weapons. On touch devices, the left side of the screen moves your character and the right side aims and fires.

Set a callsign and PIN on the login screen to keep your credits, unlocked weapons, and upgrades saved in this browser between visits.
